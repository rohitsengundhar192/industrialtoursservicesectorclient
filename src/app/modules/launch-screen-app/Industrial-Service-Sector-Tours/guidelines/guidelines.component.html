Gain real world industry exposure outside your classroom:
A typical student generates 10 to 15 kgs of waste paper in a year. An average household generates about 30 kgs of waste papers, 5-10 kgs of used clothes and 3 kgs of Rigid Plastic Waste per year.
We www.getWOW.education have initiated a WOW (Well Being of Waste) Program whereby participating institutions can engage their students in out of classroom learning experience through Industrial Tours. Industrial tours provide students with a hands-on learning experience, allowing them to see real-world applications of the concepts they are learning in the classroom. These tours offer students the opportunity to meet and interact with industry/service sector professionals, providing them with valuable networking and mentorship opportunities. Visiting different types of industrial facilities and seeing various production processes can broaden students' understanding of different industries and potential career paths. Through the WOW (Wellbeing of Waste) Program, participating institutions can engage their students in out-of-classroom learning experiences by utilizing any of several manufacturing units or service sector companies that are registered with getWOW.biz. If a student is able to collect over 10 kgs of acceptable waste, he or she is eligible for a WOW Industrial tour ticket.
WOW, Volunteer - An authorized adult staff employed by the educational institution should accompany every 10 students to monitor their activities and the safety of the students throughout their Industrial tour trip and the entry for such personnel is free.
How to participate in Industrial Tour?
STEP 1: Collect acceptable waste through the waste collection app and verify collection value credited to WOW Wallet
STEP 2: Upload the Industrial Tous participation Consent and Waiver FORM, signed by the legal guardian/parent and approved by the WOW administrator.
STEP 3: Select any Industrial Tour Site listed on the map and purchase WOW tickets by using the WOW Coins balance in WOW Wallet
STEP 4: Schedule a visit at a convenient date, get confirmation from the Industrial Tour company and proceed with the Tour on the scheduled date.
